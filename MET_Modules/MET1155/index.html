MET1155 – Dual 7‑Segment Display Module

The MET1155 board is an educational tool for truth tables, binary‑coded decimals (BCD), seven‑segment displays, and multiplexers. It integrates two red seven‑segment displays (HDSP‑H153), a BCD‑to‑7‑Seg decoder (CD74HC4511), and a 1:2 demultiplexer (SN74LVC1G19). Each display has seven independently controllable segments plus a decimal point, supports high‑visibility viewing distances, and is suitable for two‑digit apps like timers, counters, and clocks.
