Welcome

I have a passion for electronics and for teaching. My work spans embedded systems, mixed‑signal design, and hands‑on learning experiences in the lab and classroom.

Mission

In 2021 I began research into a modular, hardware‑first approach to teaching embedded systems — the Modular Embedded Tools (METs) system. The goal is to give students immediately tangible, low‑friction building blocks that connect theory to practice: logic & digital design (MET1155), and analog sensing & signal conditioning (MET2230), with assignments instructors can plug straight into their courses.
